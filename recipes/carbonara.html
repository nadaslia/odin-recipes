Carbonara

This is hands down the best carbonara I've ever eaten. Super easy to cook, quick and with few ingredients. Also a perfect meal for dinner with friends or family. So Yummy!

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of breakfast bacon
  • 500g pasta (tagiatelle or linguine recommended)
  • 4 eggs
  • 50g fresh parmesan
  • 320ml cream

Steps

  1. Remove the edges of the bacon and cut it in thin slices. Fry the bacon in a pan on medium heat till crispy. Put on kitchenpaper to soak the excess fat.
  2. Put the pasta in a big pot of boiling salt water and cook till al dente. Drain and keep it warm in the pot.
  3. In the meantime add eggs, parmesan and cream in a bowl and stirr well. Put the bacon it the same bowl and mix. Next pour the sauce over the hot pasta and merge carefully till the sauce is sticking on the noodles.
  4. On low heat let everything warm up again for like 1 1/2 minutes till the sauce thickens. Add as much black pepper as you like. Ready to serve. Buon appetito!
