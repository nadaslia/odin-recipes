5 Minute Peanut Noodles

These peanut noodles are a creamy and delicios suprise! Super quick. Super easy to cook. Super simple ingedients. Who would have thought cucumbers can be so good? Definitley not me. A meal for every time of the day that even the busiest of us can cook happily.

Ingredients

  • 1 serving of asian noodles(~80g)
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp siracha
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder and ginger powder
  • 1/3 cucumber

Steps

  1. Add the noodles in a pot of boiling saltwater and cook till ready
  2. In the meantime mix all the other ingredients, except the cucumber in a bowl. You can also add a bit of noodle water till you have a thick, creamy sauce.
  3. Cut the cucumber in veery thin slices.
  4. Mix everything together and maybe add a bit of spring onion or sesame seeds as topping. Enjoy your meal!
